## Pacing rules extracted (use these)

1. **Hook is ~30 seconds**, then a ~30-second "you're doing it wrong" reframing before any teaching. Total setup under 60 seconds.
2. **List-driven body:** 10 items, each 33–41 seconds. Average segment 37s. Nothing runs past ~45s without a visual change.
3. **Chapter cards at every section boundary** — the chapter titles are short (2–4 words after the number).
4. **Curiosity-gap chapter titles**, not descriptive ones ("Agent Hooks", not "How to configure agent hooks").
5. **Recap + next-video tease at the end** instead of a hard stop.
