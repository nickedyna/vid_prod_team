# Monetization and Compliance Notes

This repo is intended to support monetizable YouTube content, but every generated video must remain human-reviewed.

## Monetization-safe principles

- Use original scripts or substantially transformed educational commentary.
- Use your own voice whenever possible.
- Use your own screen recordings, demos, templates, and project builds.
- Use royalty-free or properly licensed music.
- Avoid reused content with no meaningful commentary.
- Avoid misleading income claims.
- Avoid promising that viewers will get a six-figure job.
- Frame salary growth as a possible path through skills, proof-of-work, positioning, and experience.

## AI disclosure and quality

AI can draft scripts, captions, metadata, and production plans. Nat remains the human reviewer and final editor.

## Claims to avoid

Do not say:

- “This guarantees a six-figure job.”
- “AI will make you rich.”
- “You can replace your job overnight.”
- “No work required.”

Preferred language:

- “This can be a practical path into enterprise marketing.”
- “Build proof-of-work that makes your value easier to understand.”
- “This is not easy, but it is learnable.”
- “I’m building this in public while still working in corporate.”

## Human review checklist

Before publishing, review:

- Script accuracy
- Captions
- Thumbnail readability
- Copyright safety
- Audio levels
- Metadata
- CTA
- Any claims about career or income
