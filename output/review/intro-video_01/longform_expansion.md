## Long script draft

If you’re trying to break into enterprise marketing, here’s the overlooked path I’m building this entire channel around: AI-powered email development. And no, I do not mean just sending newsletters.

I’m talking about enterprise email development. HTML email. QA. Personalization. Campaign briefs. Marketing automation. Analytics. AI agents. The real production systems that companies use to communicate with thousands or millions of customers every single day.

I’m Nat O’Farril. I work in enterprise marketing, and I’m building this in public while still working in corporate. I know what campaign deadlines, testing cycles, approvals, broken briefs, and last-minute changes actually look like from the inside. And what I know is this: email development is not small. Email is enterprise infrastructure, and almost every serious company needs it.

This channel is for the people who feel overlooked. Blue-collar workers trying to break into technical work. Recent grads who cannot get a real shot. Marketing people who feel stuck or invisible. Email developers who know they can become more valuable than their current role allows.

Here is the plan. We are going to learn AI, build real proof-of-work projects, and grow our value. Not with hype, and not with income guarantees, but with evidence: real systems, real templates, real workflows you can show to a hiring manager, a client, or your own leadership team.

The first system I’m building is a Brief-to-ZIP AI email workflow. A campaign brief goes in. An AI agent helps create the copy, the HTML, the QA notes, the journey logic, the deployment notes, and a full production package that ships as a ZIP. That is the kind of proof companies can understand, because it looks like the work they already do.

In upcoming videos I’ll show the first version of that workflow end to end: how a simple campaign brief becomes subject lines, a custom HTML template, a QA checklist, and a deployment-ready package. We’ll cover HTML email fundamentals, QA and rendering, personalization, automation thinking, and how to use tools like ChatGPT, GitHub Copilot, and AI agents without losing your own judgment.

The framework is simple: Learn AI. Build proof. Grow your value.

Subscribe if you want to become the AI-powered email developer companies cannot ignore.
